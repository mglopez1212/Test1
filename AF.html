We are proud supporters of Angels Forgotten!

For more information, Click Here!


Angels Forgotten was founded in April 2009, after a group visited Kenya and noticed the large population of children orphaned due to HIV. Angels Forgotten started with 10 children, by 2012 that number was at 45 children and is still growing today.

Angels Forgotten works hard to feed, clothe and educate children who are affected by this diseases so that every child can have access to a bright future within their community.
